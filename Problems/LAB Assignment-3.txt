------------------------------------------------------------------
Image Classification with MLP & Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)
------------------------------------------------------------------

Consider the following dataset.

Fashion-MNIST Dataset - Fashion Items Classification (Multiclass Classification)
This is a dataset of 60,000 28x28 grayscale images of 10 fashion categories, along with a test set of 10,000 images.

Data source in Keras: https://keras.io/api/datasets/fashion_mnist/


------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Problem-1: Multi-class Classification with MLP
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Build and train a MLP to classify the images of the Fashion-MNIST dataset.


------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Problem-2: Multi-class Classification with CNN
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Build and train a CNN to classify the images of the Fashion-MNIST dataset.
